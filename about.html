About

Our Story & Mission

Guiding Hands Placement Services was founded by Catherine Fitzgerald and Kathleen Goodwin, with over 30 years of experience in healthcare. We started the service to help families navigate complex care decisions with clarity and confidence.

Decades of Experience

Our founders saw firsthand how overwhelming care decisions can be. Guiding Hands was born from a desire to make those moments less frightening and more supported.

A Human‑First Approach

We believe in listening deeply, honoring each story and building care plans that reflect the person not just the paperwork.

Trusted Care Partners

We work only with vetted homes and caregivers who share our values of dignity, respect and safety for every senior.
